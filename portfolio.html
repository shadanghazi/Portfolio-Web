About sheddix

I help teams put AI to work.

Most AI projects stall in the same place: the prompts are vague and the context is thin, so the model gives answers that almost work. At sheddix I focus on the unglamorous part that makes everything else click — giving the model the right instructions and the right information, then building around it.

Whether it's a prompt that needs to behave, a workflow that needs structure, or a small app that needs to exist by Friday, I work hands-on with AI to get it shipped.

My goal is simple: give every client my full effort and deliver services that actually move their work forward.
